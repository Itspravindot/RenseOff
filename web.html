About Us

Welcome to Rense Off, a home-grown business born right here in Mumbai from a simple idea: cleaning should be safe. We handcraft our range of handwash, liquid detergent, dishwash, and floor cleaners by also using natural, plant-based ingredients. Our mission is to give you a powerfully clean home without using excess harsh chemicals, so you can have complete peace of mind for your family and the planet.
